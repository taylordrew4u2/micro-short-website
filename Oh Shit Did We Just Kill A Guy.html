Marcus and Eli have been best friends since the seventh grade. Tonight they’re driving home from a party that ran long and a fight that went further than either of them meant. Somewhere on the dark stretch between the freeway and the suburbs, the headlights catch something that probably wasn’t there a second ago.

What follows is four and a half minutes of the worst conversation of their lives — whispered, panicked, and occasionally, against their better judgement, very funny. Oh Shit Did We Just Kill A Guy is a micro short about the moment a friendship discovers what it’s really made of, lit by hazard lights and a flashlight that keeps dying.
